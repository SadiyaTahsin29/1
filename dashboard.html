Outcome Measures

Implementing an outcome measure system for faculty performance and upgrading can benefit students by ensuring that instructors are held accountable for their effectiveness in teaching and mentoring. This can lead to higher quality education, better learning outcomes, and a more supportive academic environment for students. Additionally, faculty upgrading can keep educators abreast of the latest developments in their field, enhancing their ability to deliver relevant and up-to-date content to students.

Purpose of the Dashboard

The primary purpose of the dashboard is to provide administrators, faculty and staff ready access to institutional data in support of all planning and assessment activities.
